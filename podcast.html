The Health and Explainable AI Podcast series embodies our vision to bridge the gap between health informatics and artificial intelligence through collaborative dialogue and shared knowledge and expertise. Health and Explainable AI Podcast, is a collaborative initiative between the Health and Explainable AI (HexAI) Research Laboratory in the Department of Health Information Management at the School of Health and Rehabilitation Sciences, and the Computational Pathology and AI Center of Excellence (CPACE) at the University of Pittsburgh School of Medicine. Led by Ahmad P. Tafti, Hooman Rashidi, and Liron Pantanowitz, the HexAI Podcast is committed to democratizing knowledge around ethical, explainable, and clinically relevant AI. Through insightful conversations with domain experts, AI practitioners, and students we’ll spotlight the latest breakthroughs, discuss real-world applications, and unpack the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead in responsible AI in healthcare. Our mission is to highlight cutting-edge research, explore practical applications, and inspire new solutions that improve healthcare outcomes using AI, and enhance the understanding of AI in medicine and healthcare.
